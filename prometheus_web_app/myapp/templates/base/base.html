About Us

  • Prometheus is the Product Management and Technology Club at XLRI.
  • A merger of a 36-year-old tech committee - SOCRATES, with ProdUX, the ProdMan Society, to meet the changing market demands of BizTech and PM roles in XLRI led to the idea of a dedicated society for the same.
